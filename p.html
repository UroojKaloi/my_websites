Bhambhore - The Ancient City

History of Bhambhore

Bhambhore is an ancient city dating back to the 1st century BC, located in Sindh, Pakistan. It is believed to be the legendary city of Debal where Muhammad bin Qasim entered Sindh in 711 AD. The ruins showcase a blend of Buddhist, Hindu, and early Islamic architecture, making it a significant archaeological site.

Activities to Do in Bhambhore

  • Explore the archaeological ruins and ancient structures.
  • Visit the Bhambhore Museum to see artifacts and historical exhibits.
  • Enjoy a scenic walk along the Indus River.
  • Learn about the legendary love story of Sassui and Punnhun.
  • Capture stunning photography of the historical sites.
