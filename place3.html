History of Keenjhar

Keenjhar Lake, also known as Kalri Lake, is one of the largest freshwater lakes in Pakistan. Located in the Thatta District of Sindh, the lake is an important source of drinking water for Karachi. It is a man-made reservoir formed by the union of two natural lakes, Keenjhar and Kalri, during the 12th century.
