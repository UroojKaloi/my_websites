Our Policies

At Sindh Tour Guide, we prioritize transparency, safety, and customer satisfaction. Please review our policies to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience while exploring Sindh.

1. Booking & Cancellation Policy

  • Advance Booking: All tours must be booked at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email with tour details after booking.
  • Cancellations & Refunds:
    • Cancellations made 48 hours before the tour will receive a full refund.
    • Cancellations within 24 hours will receive a 50% refund.
    • No refunds for last-minute cancellations or no-shows.

2. Payment Policy
